Return ONLY a valid JSON object. Do not include markdown, comments, or extra text.

  "modifiers": {
    "daily_medication": false,

Seizure type must be exactly one of:
- "UNCERTAIN": seizure type unclear, unknown, mixed but not classifiable, or not documented
- "FOCAL": focal seizures, partial seizures, focal aware, focal impaired awareness, temporal/frontal/occipital onset, secondarily generalized from focal onset
- "ABSENCE": absence seizures, staring spells, petit mal
- "MYOCLONIC": myoclonic seizures or jerks without tonic-clonic seizures
- "MYOCLONIC_ABSENCE": myoclonic and absence seizures together
- "GTCS": primary generalized tonic-clonic seizures, generalized convulsions, grand mal, no focal onset
- "GTCS_MYOCLONIC": generalized tonic-clonic and myoclonic seizures
- "GTCS_ABSENCE": generalized tonic-clonic and absence seizures
- "GTCS_MYOCLONIC_ABSENCE": generalized tonic-clonic, myoclonic, and absence seizures

Field rules:
- age: current age from the record header or clinical note, as a float. If only months are given, divide by 12.
- gender: "male" or "female".
- menopausal: "pre" if female under 50 unless postmenopause is stated; "post" if female 50 or older, postmenopause is stated, or gender is male.
- modifiers: set a modifier to true only when it is clearly documented. Do not infer undocumented comorbidities.

Modifier definitions:
- daily_medication: regular daily medication other than antiseizure drugs, oral contraceptives, or occasional PRNs.
- contraceptive: hormonal contraceptive use in a female patient. Do not count copper or levonorgestrel IUD unless the record clearly treats it as hormonal contraception.
- tumor: brain tumor requiring chemotherapy or radiation therapy.
- hepatic_failure: hepatic failure or severe liver disease.
- obesity: BMI >= 30 or documented obesity.
- diabetes: diabetes mellitus.
- bleeding: clinically significant thrombocytopenia or coagulation disorder.
- neutropenia: neutrophil count < 1500/microliter.
- renal_stone: kidney stones or renal calculi.
- allergy: documented allergy to any drug. Do not count food allergy.
- depression: depression diagnosis or active depression treatment.
- aggressive: history of irritability, aggression, behavioral dyscontrol, or clinically significant agitation.
- migraine: migraine headaches, especially frequent migraine.
- renal_failure: renal insufficiency, chronic kidney disease, or renal failure.
